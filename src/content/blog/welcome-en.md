Hello, and welcome.

This is where I collect questions worth keeping from making, technology, books, and ordinary days. Rather than trying to settle everything at once, I want to leave room for ideas to grow.

## Starting with a note

Think of this as a small digital garden. Posts do not need to be perfect; they can be revised, connected, and extended as new experiences arrive.

If you would like to exchange ideas, feel free to write to me.
